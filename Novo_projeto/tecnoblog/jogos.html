Hogwarts Legacy

Publicado em 30/01/2023

Hogwarts Legacy is an exciting upcoming open-world action-RPG set in the Harry Potter universe. The game promises to be a faithful and immersive experience for fans of the series and will be the first major video game set in the wizarding world since the release of LEGO Harry Potter in 2011. The Pros:

  • Players will be able to explore the entire Hogwarts castle and grounds.
  • will be able to attend classes with iconic characters from the series such as Professor Dumbledore, Professor Snape, and Hagrid.
  • Players will be able to cast spells, duel with other wizards, and make allies.
The Cons:
  • game has been delayed multiple times and the release date is still uncertain.
  • The game will be available only on current-generation consoles, so those with older consoles will not be able to play.
  • The game will be single-player only, so those looking for an online or co-operative experience will be disappointed.
